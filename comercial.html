Minutia Matters

Fypon products add character and beauty to both the interior and exterior of homes. Many homeowners use the exterior products to add "curb appeal" to their homes and make them more attractive. In addition to their aesthetic appeal, Fypon products are lightweight and easier to install than wood, plaster and other moulding products.

Longevity

By deincentivising insect attack and resistion water erosion Fypon® Urethane lasts for years and years.

Why Not Wood?

Fypon products are very different than wood products. They resist insect infestation, as they are not a food source for insects. Fypon products have a closed cell structure to prevent water penetration and absorption.
